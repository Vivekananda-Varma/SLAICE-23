About

SLAICE

Where creativity meets expression, and knowledge takes center stage! Our annual student magazine is a vibrant showcase of the diverse talents and perspectives of our student body. From thought-provoking articles and captivating stories to stunning artwork and photography, "Slaice" is your window into the world of our dynamic student community. Join us on this literary and artistic journey as we celebrate the voices, ideas, and talents that make our school a hub of creativity and inspiration.

Sri Aurobindo International Centre of Education

Sri Aurobindo International Centre of Education, where knowledge is not just a destination but a transformative journey. Nestled in the serene town of Puducherry, India, our center is a beacon of holistic education inspired by the teachings of Sri Aurobindo and The Mother. Here, we believe that education is a spiritual endeavor, nurturing not only the intellect but also the soul. Join us as we embark on a quest for wisdom, self-discovery, and the harmonious evolution of mind, body, and spirit.

Sri Aurobindo Ashram

the Sri Aurobindo Ashram. Situated in the tranquil and spiritually charged city of Puducherry, India, the ashram is a sanctuary for seekers on a quest for higher consciousness.
