Issue 7 / 2023

Editorial

Dear Readers,

The students of SAICE present to you the 7th edition of SLAICE. This year's edition is at variance from those previously published as it does not confine its content to a single, particular theme. Our aim this year was to bring across a myriad of articles, short stories and poems that individually hold an essence, or belong to one of the genres that literature has to offer. Our photo essays, 3D models and personal projects too convey the unique and deep passion of our contributors. This idea led us to experiment with this edition, whose outcome we leave to the readerâ€™s judgment. We hope you enjoy reading this edition as much as we enjoyed working on it.
